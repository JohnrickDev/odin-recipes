Adobo

Adobo is considered the national dish of the Philippines. It is a delicious, savory chicken dish with a garlic-y, soy sauce-based flavor, traditionally served with white rice for the whole family. It is the country's pride. With more than 7,000 islands, variations of Adobo can be found throughout the Philippines. Today, we'll be preparing the most popular and traditional Adobo recipe.
